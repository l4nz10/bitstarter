Wirh Re-Lamp you don't have to look around for your personal battery recharger anymore, just hop in your bed and place your smartphone on the Re-Lamp, and you are done!

For the ones who prefer the classic wired charge, don't worry: every Re-Lamp comes with a USB port included, so you can plug your smartphone and still have it near your bed.

Re-Lamp comes in a vast combination of colors and designs. At project launch there will be 10 models available for sell and, after a short period after launch, we will provide you a web page to create your own lamp, choosing from a vast compilation of colors, models and components from our website. Once you are done, we will ship it directly to you!

FAQ

How much will Re-Lamp cost?
The starting price will be 50$, but it will vary from model to model, due to the different components used in the costruction of the lamps.
There will be a recharging cable included in the Re-Lamp box?
Of course! Re-Lamp comes with a 1m long micro-USB cable, so you can easily use your phone while it is recharging.
